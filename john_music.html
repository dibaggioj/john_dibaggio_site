Music Information

I am primarily a jazz drumset player, but I also play a variety of world hand drums, including the pandeiro, djembe, darbuka, kanjira, riq, and tar. At a bit of an amateur level, I play two African thumb pianos: the mbira and kalimba. I mix these instruments together along with a keyboard and loop-station to perform solo live-looping. The result is music with an ambient/eclectic yet also electronic sound.

I am currently working on writing and producing some new music.
